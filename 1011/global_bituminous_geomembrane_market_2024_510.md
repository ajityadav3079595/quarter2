## What is Bituminous Geomembrane?

Bituminous Geomembranes are engineered barrier systems featuring specific impermeability requirements, installation characteristics, and durability specifications. These materials are primarily used in environmental containment applications where preventing fluid migration is critical.

These membranes are composed of bituminous binder and a framework of non-woven polyester textile, which together guarantee long-term mechanical and chemical resistance. The combination creates a robust, flexible, and impermeable barrier suitable for demanding environmental applications.
