## Market Segmentation

### By Application
- **Makeup Products**: MCC provides smooth texture, oil control, and helps in achieving matte finish. Largest application segment
- **Skincare**: Used in face masks, powders as absorbent and texture enhancer
- **Hair Care**: In dry shampoos and styling products for its oil-absorbing properties
- **Other**: Including pharmaceutical tablets where it acts as binder and disintegrant

- **Refined Cotton Based**: Higher purity, superior performance in sensitive applications like sensitive skin formulations and medical devices
